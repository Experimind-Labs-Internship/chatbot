Brand Overview

YUMI DXB Fashion is a premium women's fashion brand founded by two sisters. The brand focuses on elegant, comfortable, and high-quality fashion products such as nightwear, abayas, kaftans, and co-ord sets.

The website should provide a premium shopping experience while reflecting warmth, elegance, trust, and family values.

Project Objectives
Build customer trust through the brand story.
Showcase premium collections.
Enable online shopping.
Manage products and inventory efficiently.
Provide a smooth customer journey.
Build long-term customer relationships.
